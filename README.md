---

## Overview
This repository documents the **industrial design** of a quad-rotor **Drone for Medicine Delivery**, officially registered with the **UK Intellectual Property Office** under the **Registered Designs Act, 1949**.

The design is intended for **rapid, contactless medical logistics**, especially in emergency, rural, and hard-to-reach areas. It focuses on a compact airframe, protected rotors, and a secure medicine payload compartment.

## Design Highlights
- **Quad-rotor configuration** for stability and redundancy  
- **Encapsulated payload module** for medical safety  
- **Compact landing frame** for rough/urban surfaces  
- **Symmetric rotor placement** for balance and maneuverability  
- **Visual medical marking** for identification in emergency zones  

> See the *Representation of Designs* in this repository for multi-view renders (top, front, side, isometric).

---

## Intended Use Cases
- Emergency medicine transport  
- Rural and remote healthcare logistics  
- Hospital-to-clinic rapid delivery  
- Disaster response and relief supply drops
